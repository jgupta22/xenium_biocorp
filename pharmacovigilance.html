Patient Safety & Pharmacovigilance

Xenium Biocorp is committed to patient safety and responsible product monitoring. We encourage the reporting of adverse events, product complaints, or safety-related concerns.

Report an Event

If you experience or witness an adverse event related to a Xenium Biocorp product, please report it immediately.

Please include:

  • Product name
  • Batch number (if available)
  • Description of the event
  • Patient demographics (Age/Gender only)
Email: xeniumbiocorp@gmail.com

Important Information

Confidentiality: All reports are handled confidentially and reviewed in coordination with our manufacturing partners where required.

Medical Advice: We cannot provide medical advice. If you have a medical emergency, please contact your doctor or visit the nearest hospital immediately.

Data Privacy: Please do not share sensitive patient-identifying information (such as patient names) through website forms or unencrypted email.
