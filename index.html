About Xenium Biocorp

Our Story

Founded 2 decades ago by Birbal Gupta and Kuldeep Singh, Xenium Biocorp has a long-standing tradition of serving communities across Rajasthan. We operate on a lean model, partnering with certified contract manufacturers to ensure high-quality production while focusing on effective marketing and distribution. Founder, Birbal Gupta has been a pioneer in the pharmaceutical industry for more than 40 years.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide accessible, high-quality pharmaceutical products that improve the health and well-being of individuals and families throughout India.

Vision: To be the leading pharmaceutical company in India, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Approach

We believe in ethical practices, strong partnerships, and a deep understanding of the needs of our customers and healthcare professionals. Our focus is on quality assurance from manufacturing to delivery, ensuring that every product meets the highest standards of safety and efficacy.
