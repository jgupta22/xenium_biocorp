Quality & Compliance

Uncompromising Standards for Third-Party Manufactured Pharma Products

At Xenium Biocorp, quality is a shared responsibility. While manufacturing is carried out by certified third-party partners, Xenium maintains oversight across key stages to ensure consistency, compliance, and traceability.

  • 1

    Partner Qualification

    We work only with licensed, GMP-compliant manufacturing partners who meet our stringent standards.

  • 2

    Batch Documentation

    Internal review of essential manufacturing and quality records before market release.

  • 3

    Packaging Controls

    Verification of packaging materials, labeling accuracy, and tamper-proof sealing.

  • 4

    Post-Market Monitoring

    Active tracking of market feedback, complaints, and product safety data.
