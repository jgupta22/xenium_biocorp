Decades of Trust in Pharma Distribution

Our Story

Xenium Biocorp was founded with the objective of serving regional healthcare markets with reliability and integrity. As a prominent pharma company in Jaipur, we grew by understanding local distribution realities and staying close to the needs of pharmacists and clinicians across Rajasthan.

Instead of investing in large manufacturing facilities, we chose a focused model. Xenium works with certified third-party manufacturers while retaining oversight on partner selection and quality, allowing us to operate efficiently as a modern pharma marketing company in India.
