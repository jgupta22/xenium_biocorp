About Xenium Biocorp

Xenium Biocorp is an India-based pharmaceutical marketing and distribution company with a strong presence across Rajasthan. For over two decades, we have focused on building dependable access to quality medicines through long-term relationships.

Our Story

Xenium Biocorp was founded with the objective of serving regional healthcare markets with reliability and integrity. Starting from Rajasthan, the company grew by understanding local distribution realities and staying close to the needs of pharmacists and clinicians. Instead of investing in large manufacturing facilities, we chose a focused model. Xenium works with certified third-party manufacturers while retaining oversight on partner selection and quality.

Our Approach

  • Focused Portfolio: We build and market a selective range of formulations that address everyday clinical needs.
  • Quality Oversight: We maintain internal checks across partner qualification, batch document review, and packaging verification.
  • Ethical Marketing: Sustainable growth comes from ethical promotion and clear communication.

Our Vision

To become a trusted regional pharmaceutical brand known for dependable supply, responsible marketing, and consistent product quality.

Manufacturing & Quality

Xenium Biocorp does not operate its own manufacturing facilities. All products are manufactured by licensed third-party manufacturers that follow established quality systems. Before release, we review key batch and packaging documentation.

Quality & Compliance

At Xenium Biocorp, quality is a shared responsibility. While manufacturing is carried out by certified third-party partners, Xenium maintains oversight across key stages to ensure consistency, compliance, and traceability.

  • Partner Qualification: We work only with licensed, GMP-compliant manufacturing partners.
  • Batch Documentation: Internal review of essential manufacturing and quality records before market release.
  • Packaging Controls: Verification of packaging materials and labeling accuracy.
  • Post-Market Monitoring: Active tracking of market feedback and product safety.
